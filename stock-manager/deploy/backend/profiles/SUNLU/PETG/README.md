## Recommended Build Plate

| Plate | Glue? | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Textured PEI | No | Best option — good adhesion + easy release |
| Cool Plate (unheated) | Yes | Glue required per TDS |
| Engineering Plate | No | Works but may over-adhere |

## Notes

- This is the **standard** SUNLU PETG (not High Speed)
- Lower speeds than PETG HS but still good up to 200mm/s at 240-260°C
- TDS test specimens printed at 150mm/s, 255°C, 100% infill
- Product yellows under UV — store away from direct sunlight
